# Business Analysis Portfolio

This repository showcases business-focused analytics projects across Tableau, Power BI, and other BI tools.

Each project emphasizes:
- Problem framing
- Metric design
- Insight generation
- Business recommendations

## Projects

### Year on Year  (Tableau)
- Add Year-on-Year business performance dashboard with KPI tracking and category-level profit analysis (Superstore)
- Key insight: Looking at H2 vs PY, Growth is strong YTD (Orders up +12% | Shipments +9%) , but it’s not translating cleanly into profitability (flat at ~1%) — and that disconnect is category-driven.
- [View Project](https://github.com/allisonljones-code/business_analysis/tree/main/Tableau/Year%20on%20Year%20Business%20Performance)
### Customer Segmentation (Tableau)
- Segmented customers into 4 quadrants based on order volume and profitability
- Key insight: Majority fall into low-value segment → long tail risk
- [View Project](https://github.com/allisonljones-code/business_analysis/tree/main/Tableau/Sales%20and%20Customer%20Performance)
